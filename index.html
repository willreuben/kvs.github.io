SOMERLED
SOMERLED IS THE GRADUATE COLLECTION FROM FASHION DESIGNER KRISTIE V SHERYN

SOMERLED IS A COLLECTION THAT EMBODIES FREEDOM AND AUTHENTICITY, WITHOUT EMPHASIS ON SEXUALITY NOR MASCULINITY, IN ORDER TO FORM A MODERN AESTHETIC OF A BONDARYLESS MAN THAT STRIVES FOR BETTER, WITHOUT CONSTRAINTS OF SOCIETAL PRESSURE.

WEAVING TOGETHER SILHOUETTES OF TRADITIONAL NORSE PAGAN DRESS AND THE HYPER FEMININE DRESS OF THE 1970s. THIS BLEND OF SILHOUETTES IS VERY IMPORTANT AT ADDING CONTEXT TO THE DESIGNS AND HELPS FORGE MY VISION OF HOW I, AS A DESIGNER, INTERPRET A NEW FORM OF MASCULINITY.
